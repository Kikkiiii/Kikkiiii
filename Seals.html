Where do seals live?

Seals can be found along most coasts and cold waters, but most of them live in the Arctic and Antarctic waters.

Fun Facts:

  1. There are two types of seals - the earless and the eared seals.
  2. Seals sleep on land and in the water.
  3. Seals know how to hold their breath! They can do this for up to 30min and elephant seals up to 2 hours!
  4. Seals are known as the ‘dogs of the sea’ they are curious, playful and sociable creatures.
  5. Seals are very chatty, they like to bark, growl, grunt, chirp, click, and even whistle
